The Extension Customer Email Notifications is enabled on your account.

This extension has the capability to send to customers email notifications when a new subscription has been activated for them.

You can configure for which products as well as the content of the emails on the extension options available on main navigation menu.

Please note that all the email notifications will be sent as and with the email address .

In the use case you would like to change that please configure a different one using the branding options of CloudBlue Connect
